# Maintainers

Current maintainers of Caladon. See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) for the decision
model (lazy consensus, with two‑maintainer approval required for changes touching the
security model, attestation logic, the cryptographic data plane, or the frozen
`contracts/`).

| Maintainer | Role | GitHub |
|---|---|---|
| Josh Bilson | Project lead | [@joshbilson](https://github.com/joshbilson) |

Maintainership is earned through a sustained track record of good contributions and
sound judgment — especially catching overclaims, plaintext‑exposure paths, and
attestation gaps. Any maintainer may nominate a contributor (see GOVERNANCE.md §3).
